Birthday Cake Survival

Map Info:
Survive on a cake and enter the cake factory in this unique and colorful survival / adventure map. This map features various cakes, and the cake factory. Its your job to stop the factory from using sheep into sprinkles.
What is Birthday Cake Survival?
Birthday Cake Survival is a survival map, in which you survive on a birthday cake made out of wool. The only wood you receive at the start is in the candle, and the rest of the supplies are scattered in other places. There is one objective though, which is to free the sheep that the Sprinkles Factory are keeping. Killing sheep, making the sheep into sprinkles, and selling those sprinkles to blind people, is what that factory does. Its your job to stop them. (And survive of course.)
